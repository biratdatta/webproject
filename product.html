What is PM?

I had no clue what "product management" was one year ago. When I arrived at Babson College, I thought my career path would be that of an entrepreneur -- starting and running my own business. However, I found that I expecially enjoyed the ideation and design phase of a start-up. It is during the phase where anything and any solution is possible. I would like to think of myself as a creative person, and any problem I encounter, I try to find a different and more effective solution.

That is what product management is -- identifying a problem, researching all variables, and determining a solution. Product managers are the center of satisfying customers, developing the technology, and serving the business. This is a career path I am more than proud to be on.
